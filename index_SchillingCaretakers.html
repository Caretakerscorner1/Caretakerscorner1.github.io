Welcome to Caretakers Corner

Have you had a loved one unexpectedly become disabled?

I did.

My name is Elizabeth Schilling and I hope that by sharing my story, it will help you or a loved one going through a similar experience. There were so many things that I did not know or understand.

In September, 2019, my mom and dad suffered strokes on the same day. I discovered that I was completely unprepared for this event. I was very close to my parents. My dad came home from the hospital and died a month later. My mom was still in the hospital at the time of his death.

As a result of the stroke, my mom went from being a mobile, lively individual to a paralyzed, bedridden individual. She lost her husband of 60 years and her ability to care for herself. I want to share some information with you that I learned going through this journey. Hopefully, it will help you through the challenges you might experience as a person who has been suddenly thrown into a similar life-changing event.

“Caregiving often calls us to lean into love we didn’t know possible.” - Tia Walker
