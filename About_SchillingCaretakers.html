About Me

I am a person who went from being a loving daughter of an independent parent to becoming the loving caretaker of a paralyzed dependent elderly parent. Although, I have a law degree, a master's degree in public health and have taken a lot of courses in many subjects, I felt utterly unprepared for this new role.

In September, 2019, my mom and dad suffered strokes on the same day. I discovered that I was completely unprepared for this event. I want to share some information with you that I discovered going through this new life experience. I hope that my life experience will give you some insights on your journey. Please take comfort in knowing that you are not alone.

“Education is not preparation for life; education is life itself.” - John Dewey

Author: Elizabeth Schilling, JD, MPH
