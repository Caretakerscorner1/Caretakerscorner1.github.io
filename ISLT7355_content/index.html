Weclome to Caretakers Corner

By: Elizabeth Schilling


Have you had a loved one unexpectedly become disabled? I did. I became a caretaker. If you are a caretaker-this website is for you.


This is an educational website. Its purpose is to help caretakers and family members of stroke victims by providing educational resources in their time of need. If you are going through this type of struggle, please know that you are not alone!


I hope that by telling our story, it will help you through the challenges you might experience as a person who has been suddenly thrown into a similar life-changing event.
